    🎭 False Positive Alerts — City Analysis

    A false positive occurs when a city is listed in a flash warning (מבזק) but does not appear in the subsequent real rocket/drone alert. These residents were told to prepare but no threat reached their area.
    FP rate = false positive occurrences ÷ total flash appearances for that city.
